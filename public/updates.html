SCORE PROTECTION ENGINE

SMART SYNC ENGINE
Auto-syncs after every game when signed in. Merges data intelligently across devices — union of scores by timestamp, best streaks, earliest badges. localStorage stays truth during play; the server is your backup.

RISK NUDGE ENGINE
“Protect your scores” prompt appears when you have a streak worth saving — 7+ day streak, 5+ games played, or 3+ badges earned. Never nags, only nudges when it matters.

—

Fixed 2 dots placed at unreachable positions in the maze (row 9-10, column 20). These cells were surrounded by walls on all sides — impossible to eat. MARCH 9, 2026
BUGFIX — Mine Finder AI
—
MINE FINDER AI FIX BUGFIX
Fixed two AI bugs: 1) When multiple cells had equal mine probability, the AI always picked the first one (lowest grid index) instead of choosing randomly — making it predictable and sometimes appearing stuck. Now randomly selects among tied candidates. 2) After flagging cells, the AI would stop instead of re-analyzing the board with the new flags, missing deductions that the flags unlocked.
"The AI learned that when you can't decide, flip a coin — don't just freeze."
